Our Photo Booth Solutions

Cutting-edge technology for unforgettable moments

360° Video Booth

Our signature 360° video booth creates immersive slow-motion videos that capture every angle of your special moment. Guests step onto an elevated platform while our camera rotates around them, creating stunning cinematic videos that are instantly shareable.

Perfect For:

Weddings, corporate events, product launches, milestone celebrations, and red-carpet experiences.

Open Air Digital Photo Booth

Our modern, open-concept photo booth allows for maximum guest interaction and group photos. Featuring a high-resolution camera, professional lighting, and instant printing, this versatile option is perfect for any event type and space.

Perfect For:

All event types, especially those with large groups or limited space requirements.

Video Confession Booth

Give your guests a private space to record heartfelt video messages. Our Video Confession Booth creates a comfortable, semi-private environment where guests can leave personal messages for the hosts. Perfect for weddings, anniversaries, and retirement parties.

Perfect For:

Weddings, milestone birthdays, anniversaries, retirement parties, and corporate testimonials.

Professional DSLR Camera Photo Booth

For those who demand the highest quality images, our Professional DSLR setup delivers magazine-quality photos. Using top-of-the-line Canon or Nikon cameras paired with professional lighting, this option provides stunning, frame-worthy prints and digital images.

Perfect For:

Luxury events, fashion-forward gatherings, corporate headshots, and high-end weddings.
